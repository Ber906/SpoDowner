How SpoDown Works

Simple Music Downloads in 3 Steps

SpoDown lets you download your favorite songs, albums, and playlists from Spotify with just a few clicks. Here's how it works:

1

Copy the Spotify URL

Find the song, album, or playlist you want to download on Spotify. Click the share button and copy the URL to your clipboard.

2

Paste the URL in SpoDown

Come back to SpoDown and paste the URL into the download box. Click the "Download Music" button to start the process.

3

Download Your Music

SpoDown will process your request and provide you with downloadable audio files. It's that simple!
